Lasagna


This is a recipe for lasagna that I do not recommend, as I am only writing it to test my HTML chops


Ingredients

  • water
  • pasta sheets
  • meat (preferably ground)
  • tomato sauce
  • pan of sorts
  • oven
  1. turn on oven
  2. boil water
  3. put sheet pasta in water
  4. cook meat in tomato sauce
  5. put cooked pasta sheets in pan
  6. put tomato meat on pasta sheets
  7. put ricotta cheese on tomato meat
  8. put mozarella cheese on ricotta cheese
  9. repeat as often as desired
  10. put pan in oven
  11. take pan out of oven
